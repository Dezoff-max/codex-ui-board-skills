# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project aims to be a welcoming, practical, and professional open-source space. Contributors are expected to communicate with respect and focus on improving the work.

Examples of positive behavior:

- giving constructive feedback
- explaining tradeoffs clearly
- welcoming different experience levels
- keeping discussions focused on the project
- crediting useful ideas and contributions

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- publishing private information
- repeated off-topic disruption
- bad-faith or intentionally misleading contributions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that do not meet this standard. Repeated or severe violations may result in a contributor being blocked from participating.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant, adapted into a concise project-specific version.
